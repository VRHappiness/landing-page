VR Happiness

VR happiness is an episodic software game that sets out to revolutionize Social Emotional Learning in classroomsby allowing educators to teach SEL skills the treasure hunt way, combining real life interaction with digital content, VR and AR. This product focuses on developing the core students’ 21st century skills, namely problem-solving, innovation and creativity, social skills, teamwork, health and wellness and ICT skills.

How does it work ?

1.Teacher

The teacher starts playing on the projector an episode depicting a story.

2.Character

At times, a funny character pops in and asks children to help the kid from the story by embarking in a treasure hunt.

3.Kid

For this, they will need to find clues they need to discover by playing some games or cooperating in teams to find the hidden clues in AR mode. When they all solve the quest, they go in VR mode to experience emotions in a fun safe way.
